# Drive Rush

Perform a quick rush forward from a Drive Parry or a cancelable normal attack. Drive Rush from a [[Lesson 3.5 - More Drive System Mechanics#Drive Parry|parry]] costs 1 Drive Stock, while Drive Rush from a normal attack costs 3 Drive Stocks.

## Simple Drive Rush Combo

> [!Combo]
> =:2MP/2MK > DR 5HP > 4MP > 4HK > j.MP > j.214K:=
