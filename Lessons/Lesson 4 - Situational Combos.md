# Lesson 4 - Situational Combos

## Jump In Combos

## Combos into Super

### Level 1

Some already mentioned combos in [[Lesson 4 - Corner Combos#In the corner (Side switch)|Side Switch Combos]]:

> [!Combo]
> =:2MP/2MK > DR 4HK > [6]> 4HK > 236[HK] > 236236K:= 

> [!Combo]
> =:j.214K > 4MP > 4HK > DR 4MP > 236[HK] > 236236K:= 

> [!Combo]
> =:2MP/2MK > DR 2HP > 4HK > 214LP > 236236K:=

### Level 2

Don't really use level 2, but you can always take the 2MP/2MK BnB combo and stop early  to get:

> [!combo]
> =: 2MP/2MK > DR 5HP > 4MP~HK > 214214P:=

### Level 3

> [!Combo]
> =:CH 5HP > 214HP > 5LK > 236MK/236236K/236236P:=

> [!Combo]
> =:j.HP > 5HP > DR 4HK, DR 5HP > 214MP > DR 5HP, 623[HK] > 236236P:=

## Corner Combos

### Offensive

> [!combo]
> =: (whiff LP) > CH 5MP > 5HP~HK > 236LK > 623MK:=
> A great tool in the corner when your opponent is mashing.  Only works off of counter-hit.  Use the whiffed =:LP:= to setup and hit =:5MP:= frame 1

### Defensive (Side-Switch)

These combos are used when *you're* stuck in the corner and need to get out

> [!Combo]
> =:2MP/2MK > DR 4HK > [6]> 4HK > 236[HK] > 236236K:= 

> [!Combo]
> =:j.214K > 4MP > 4HK > DR 4MP > 236[HK] > 236236K:=
