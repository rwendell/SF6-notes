# Drive Impact

A powerful strike that can absorb an opponent’s incoming attack. Perform this on an opponent backed into the corner to induce a wall splat, even if they block the attack.

## Input

Input ![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/8/88/SF6_HP.png/22px-SF6_HP.png)+![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/8/8f/SF6_HK.png/22px-SF6_HK.png) (HP+HK) to perform.

# Drive Parry

Automatically repel an opponent’s attack and replenish Drive when performed successfully. Perform a Perfect Parry by parrying just before an opponent’s attack hits you.

## Input

Hold ![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/9/9b/SF6_MP.png/22px-SF6_MP.png)+![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/1/15/SF6_MK.png/22px-SF6_MK.png) (MP+MK) to perform.

# Drive Reversal

Perform a counterattack while blocking an opponent’s attack. The damage is low but can help you out of tight situations when you’re being pressured.

## Input

Press ![22](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/3/3d/F.png/22px-F.png)+![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/8/88/SF6_HP.png/22px-SF6_HP.png)+![](https://wiki.supercombo.gg/images/thumb/8/8f/SF6_HK.png/22px-SF6_HK.png) (6HPHK) during blockstun to perform
